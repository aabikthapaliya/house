## ✅ What "Everything" Means

On YOUR week, you do ALL of this on your day off:

### 🧹 Vacuum
- All rooms, hallways, common areas
- Under furniture, corners, under tables
- Focus on high-traffic areas (kitchen, lounge, hall)

### 🧽 Mop
- All hard floors — kitchen, hallways, bathroom floors
- Use fresh water + floor cleaner
- Wring mop properly — wet floors = slip hazard

**Mop Bucket Rules:**
- 🟢 Bucket A = Kitchen floors ONLY
- 🔴 Bucket B = Toilet floors ONLY
- ⚠️ NEVER cross-use — bacteria from toilets should not touch kitchen floors

### 🍳 Kitchen Deep Clean
- Wipe all counters and stovetop
- Clean sink and faucet
- Sweep kitchen floor
- Wipe down fridge front / handles
- Check for expired food, throw out old stuff

### 🚽 Toilets
- Scrub bowls, clean seats, wipe handles
- Clean mirrors, wipe shelves
- Sweep + mop toilet floor
- Empty small bins if full

### 🐕 Dogs (if applicable)
- Clean up any dog mess
- Wipe areas they frequent

## 📌 Daily Baseline Rules (Everyone — Every Day)

These are NOT part of "your week" — these are YOUR responsibility every single day:

1. **Dishes** — Wash your own, don't leave in sink overnight
2. **Bench** — Wipe after cooking, put stuff back
3. **Toilet** — Flush every time, wipe seat if you splash, replace TP when empty
4. **Bathroom** — Hang your towel, don't leave hair in drain
5. **Bins** — If bin is full, take it out — don't pile on top
6. **Guests** — Whoever has the guest cleans up after them
7. **General** — Put things back where you found them

3. Persistent skipping → **house meeting** — just fix it, no beef
